PulseRoot Verification

Let people know you're the real deal.

What's this?

A verification program. If you're a content creator, a community manager, or whatever, we welcome you to PulseRoot! You probably want your fans to know you're the real thing, right? Well we do that pretty simply: a badge on your profile. For example, I have a developer badge. You'll get a fancy verification badge. Ooh!

What are the requirements?

Well, we need to know you're the real person, obviously. So you need:

  1. Some sort of reputation
  2. Proof - a simple third-party connection will do.
  3. A PulseRoot account, obviously.
  4. A Discord; this is essential so we have an easy way of contacting you.
  5. If you're known for making web applications, your code needs to be open-sourced.
