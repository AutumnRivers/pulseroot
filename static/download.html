PulseRoot Desktop

Secure, fast, reliable chat app. No browser required.

Why should I get this?

  • Internal Browser
    • PulseRoot Desktop comes with an internal browser built for privacy. With Privacy Badger and HTTPS Everywhere included by default, you never need to worry about your privacy again.
  • Mojave Dark Mode
    • Apple users - you get a special treat! PulseRoot's dark mode will activate automatically with your Mojave!
  • No Browser Required
    • Internal browser, chat at your fingertips, and easy profile customization? You may as well just throw away your Chrome or Firefox! (Don't actually throw away your Firefox, it's useful)
  • New Modern Chat Theme
    • PulseRoot's chatrooms got a UI overhaul - only on the desktop app!
  • Custom Clients Supported
    • We allow modified clients! So if you want to make something like BetterPulseRoot, just run it through Smartie.
  • Connect to IRC chatrooms
    • Easily connect to IRC servers with IRC Mode, a feature exclusive to the desktop application.
