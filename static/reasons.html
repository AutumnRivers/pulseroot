Securing an account

You've got reason to.

PulseRoot's global chat can be used without an account, yes, but you should really secure an account. But why? I can already hear you saying. Well, many reasons.

Benefits of creating an account:

Profile Customization
Signing up for an account means you get your own profile. With this profile, you can have a custom name color, custom display name, profile picture, and more!
Private Messaging
If you have an account, you can start and engage in private messages with other registered users. Neato!
Chat Perks
As a guest, you can't do much. And in chat, you appear as a normal Guest with nothing special to you, which is boring. When you have an account, you'll gain a unique look in chat, along with your name linking directly to your profile.
Cut Down On Impersonation
Securing a username means nobody else can use it—it's all yours! Make it so your fans can find you on PulseRoot. While you're at it, just in case, why not sign up for the Verification Program?
Set Up Privacy
You like privacy? What a coincidence! We do too! Creating an account allows you to configure a whole slew of privacy settings.
Theming
As a guest, you don't get theming. Sad. But as a user, you do get theming. Nice. No external add-ons required, you can even easily create your own custom theme if that's your thing. (Nerd)
Desktop Application
If you ever plan on using the desktop app for PulseRoot, then you may wish to sign up. You can't use it a guest.

Cons of creating an account:

You'll make your friends who haven't made a PulseRoot account yet jealous. Yikes!
Sorry about that.
