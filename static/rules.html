PulseRoot Rules

Yes, we have rules.

No drama
We don't like drama, and it always causes problems. Leave your drama at the door. Inciting drama also falls under this rule.
No harrassment
Treat others how you want to be treated! If someone asks you to stop, stop!
No illegal activity
Illegal actions include, but are not limited to: doxxing, child pornography (yes, this includes loli hentai and cub porn), setting up drug deals, etc.
No NSFW in the global chat
You're free to send that in the NSFW chat, but nowhere else.
No begging for mod/admin
Trust me when I say you will not get it that way.
Use common sense
If you don't think you can do it, then don't!

On the subject of: Bans & Transparency

There are three types of bans you can get on PulseRoot: Restriction Ban, Account Ban, and IP Ban.

Restriction Ban
This is a very light ban - so light that it doesn't even lock you out of your account. When applied, it kicks you out of your account, but you're free to log back in. However, you may come back with restrictions in place. This can include: increased cooldown, disabled markdown, etc.
Account Ban
This is the typical ban. You'll get locked out of your account. You may use the global chat as a guest, but that is all.
IP Ban
This is a very rare ban, but a ban that can happen nonetheless. IF you are IP banned, your IP is banned and you will be prevented from making accounts and viewing the global chat. In extreme cases, you may also be prevented from visiting any part of PulseRoot.

Transparency is a big thing at PulseRoot. If you're ever unsure about something, you're free to ask any member of the team.
